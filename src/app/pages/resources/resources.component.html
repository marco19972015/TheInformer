Make The Road New York
https://maketheroadny.org/

Make the Road New York provides legal services and support for immigrant communities in New York. They offer assistance with immigration applications, workers' rights, and housing issues.

Catholic Migration Services
https://catholicmigration.org/

Catholic Migration Services offers immigration legal services to low-income immigrants in Brooklyn and Queens. They assist with green card applications, DACA, and more.

National Immigrant Justice Center
https://immigrantjustice.org/es

NIJC provides legal services for immigrants and refugees across the United States, and they offer some resources in Spanish

The Legal Aid Society
https://legalaidnyc.org/es/

The Legal Aid Society in New York offers legal services to immigrants, including representation in immigration court and assistance with various immigration matters
